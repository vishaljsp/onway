About BHMS:

Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (B.H.M.S.) is an undergraduate degree program in Homeopathy. This is a 5½ year degree course which includes 4 and ½ years of academic sessions and 1½ year internet ship with practical knowledge.
There are also some universities that provides BHMS through distance learning courses.

 

Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS) Eligibility :

Applicants looking for a BAMS must have fulfilled the eligibility criteria mentioned below.

Intermediate 10+2 or equivalent examination with Science (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology) as mandatory subjects.
A minimum aggregate score of 50% at both Higher & Senior Secondary levels (classes 10th & 12th), and at the level of graduation.
The age limit for the BHMS program is 20 years for General category and 24 years for the reserved category.

BHMS Admission Process:

Admission to various BHMS institutes is done through the entrance exam. Candidates need to go through different entrance exams conducted at the national level and state level.

Selection is based on marks obtained in the respective entrance exam, followed by a round of Group Discussion and Personal Interview. With these rounds, candidates are shortlisted. After completing all the rounds successfully, there is a final interview, and seats are allocated as per the performance in the examination.

Scope & Future of Homeopathy

A rising number of people in India are preferring to use homeopathic medicines over conventional medicines. Most homeopathic doctors open private clinics or work in a multi-specialty chain of homeopathic clinics.

There is a lot of career choice after completing BHMS. He can join a medical institution or hospital in private as well as government sector. Or can further pursue higher studies.

If you like teaching and are passionate about educating people about Homeopathy then there is a satisfactory career in teaching as well. The teaching field is also open for BHMS graduates.
