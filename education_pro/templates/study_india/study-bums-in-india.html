About Study BUMS in India:

BUMS (Bachelor of Unani System of Medicine & Surgery ) is an undergraduate course in Unani Medicine. It is a five years duration of course including a 1-year of the internship. It is the fourth most popular medication system in India after the allopathic, ayurvedic, and homeopathic system. This medication system is based on four elements in the human body the Blood, Phlegm, yellow bile, and the black bile.

Bachelor of Unani System of Medicine & Surgery (BUMS) Eligibility :

Applicants looking for a BUMS must have fulfilled the eligibility criteria mentioned below.

Intermediate 10+2 or equivalent examination with Science (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology) as mandatory subjects.
A minimum aggregate score of 50% at Senior Secondary level (Class 12th)
The students who have studied Urdu in 12th class or having proficiency in the Urdu language has given the preference in admission.

 

BUMS Admission Process in India:

Admission is done on the basis of NEET (UG) Exam. It is now compulsory to clear NEET for admission into many medical courses, including BUMS.

After scores are obtained in the NEET, centralized counseling takes place which decides your seat based on performance. Colleges may take personal interview round afterward to further screen the students.
